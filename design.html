Soft Body Controller

I designed my plush controller using a Flora, conductive fabric, and conductive thread. I attached my conductive fabric using iron-on interfacing to my fabric, which was cut out into the shape of my plush controller. I had to sew the thread through the fabric and then through a pin on the Flora. The fabric was cut into the shape of an up and down arrow, similar to buttons on a keyboard. Everything fit into my plush controller, and it was a lot of fun to build.
